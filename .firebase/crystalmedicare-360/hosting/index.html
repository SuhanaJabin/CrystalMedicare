ABOUT

YOUR HEALTH IS OUR PRIORITY

Welcome to CRYSTAL MEDI CARE,where we believe in providing comprehensive and compassionate healthcare services to meet your needs. With a dedicated team of professionals,and a commitment to your well-being, we’re here to support you on your journey to a healthier life.

SERVICES

1

Caregivers

Our compassionate caregivers provide personalised assistance with daily activities,companionship and emotional support, ensuring seniors feel comfortable and cared for in their own homes or in care facilities.

2

Skilled Nursing

Our qualified nurses deliver expert medicalcare, medication mangement,and monitoring of health conditions, promoting optimal health and wellbeing for seniors with complex medical needs.

3

Senior Care

We offer a range of senior care services tailored to meet the unique needs of each individual,including assistance with personal hygiene,meal preparation,mobility support,and more,fostering independence and dignity.

4

Kitchen Staff

Our kitchen assistants help with meal planning, grocery shopping, and meal preparation, ensuring seniors recieve nutritious and delicious meals that meet their dietary preferences and requirements.

5

24/7 Home Care Services

We provide comprehensive home care services around the clock, ensuring continuous support and care for seniors and their families whenever needed.

6

Cleaning Staff

Our dedicated cleaning staff maintain a clean and hygienic environment in care homes and private residences, ensuring a safe and comfortable living space for seniors and their caregivers.
